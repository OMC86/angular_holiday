Accommodation Options

Emerald Travel Montserrat offers luxury accommodation in handpicked villas with first class facilities. Our villas provide you with comfort and convenience against a beautiful backdrop and lavish surroundings that you can make your home.

The package is inclusive of an ensuite double room within a luxury villa per traveller, unless booked as two persons sharing. Daily breakfast is included. Villas include communal living room, kitchen, with self-catering facilities and swimming pool. Only travellers booked with Emerald Travel Montserrat are accommodated at the designated villas.

Double rooms are provided as standard. Requests for twin rooms should be made with the booking. If you prefer something a little more private or tailored to your needs, Emerald Travel work closely with local hotels and will endeavour to meet your request.
