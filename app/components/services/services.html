Our Services

Emerald Travel MONTSERRAT aim to promote the beauty, nature and culture within the Caribbean island of Montserrat. We offer tailor made package holidays for travellers searching for unique Caribbean culture, natural flora and fauna, the ability to explore an active volcano and St Patricks festivities.

The Package

  • 10 nights inclusive Caribbean experience in Montserrat during the St Patricks day celebrations.
  • Travelling from 8th March – 18th March 2019.
  • Return travel from UK/Ireland to Montserrat, via Antigua.
  • Transfers
  • Day trip in Antigua
  • Luxury Bed & Breakfast Accommodation
  • 10 daily activities to explore and experience the island, the volcano and the people
  • Beach BBQ finale

Price

We are competitively priced.

For those travelling from the UK, the total cost of the package per person is £1900. A discounted rate is offered for two persons sharing a room at a total cost of £1700 per person.

For those travelling from Ireland, the total cost of the package per person is £2000. A discounted rate is offered for two persons sharing a room at a total cost of £1800 per person.

Accomodation

The package is inclusive of an ensuite double room within a luxury villa per traveller, unless booked as two persons sharing. Daily breakfast is included. Villas include communal living room, kitchen, with self-catering facilities and swimming pool.
