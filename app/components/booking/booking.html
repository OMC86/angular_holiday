Bookings

To book with Emerald Travel and secure your place for the next trip to Montserrat contact Rochelle Lee at info@emeraldtravel.org or call on 07944342113.

The basic cost of your tailor made holiday includes:

  • Return travel from the UK or Ireland to Montserrat, via Antigua.
  • Transfers
  • Antigua Day tour
  • Luxury accommodation for 10 nights with breakfast
  • Daily activities for the 10 night duration

An initial deposit of £300 per person is required together with the Booking Form. We aim to confirm bookings within a week, with many taking just a day or two. You will receive a confirmation invoice once your booking has been made and further details for you to prepare for your holiday. The balance payment is due 12 weeks before departure date.

It is essential, and a condition of booking, that you protect yourself with a suitable travel insurance policy as soon as you book a trip. You will be asked to provide proof of insurance prior to departure.
