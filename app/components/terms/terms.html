An initial deposit of £300 per person is required together with the Booking Form. You will receive a confirmation invoice once your booking has been made. The balance payment is due 12 weeks before departure date. A staged payment plan can be agreed when booking.

If you are booking within 12 weeks of the departure date the full amount is payable. If the monies are not paid in time, we reserve the right to cancel your holiday and apply the cancellation conditions as set out in Paragraph 7.

3. THE PACKAGE

The basic cost of your tailor made holiday includes:

  • Return travel from UK/Ireland to Montserrat, via Antigua.
  • Transfers
  • Antigua day tour
  • Luxury Accommodation for 10 nights with breakfast
  • Daily activities for the 10 night duration

4. TRAVEL

Flight date and time are fixed and detailed on your travel itinerary.

Flights from London to Antigua are operated by Virgin Atlantic. Flights from Dublin to London are operated by Air Lingus. A ticket will be issued in the name of individual passengers on the booking. Conditions of carriage from the airline operator are transferred to ticket holders. Please review the relevant conditions prior to travel.

Ferry from Antigua to Montserrat operates in conjuction with the Government of Montserrat. Terms and conditions for carriage of passengers and baggage are transferred to ticket holders. Please review prior to travel.

Transfers include return travel from the St Johns airport, Antigua to Heritage Quay port, Antigua; return travel from Little Bay port, Montserrat to your accommodation and travel from your accommodation to activities specified in the Emerald Travel Montserrat itinerary.

5. CANCELLATION BY YOU

You may cancel your holiday at any time, provided that the person signing the booking form confirms this in writing. Your cancellation is taken from the date we receive your letter or email (info@emeraldtravel.org).

As cancellation incurs administrative costs and many suppliers require advance payments, we will apply cancellation charges.

Cancellations made within 14 days of the booking date and at least 42 days in advance of the departure date will be 100% refunded. After this period booking deposit are non-refundable.

Additional charges are applied for cancellations closer to departure.

Cancellations made in the period 22 - 42 days before departure will incur 50% charge of the holiday price.

Cancellations made within 21 days of departure will incur 100% charge of the holiday price.
